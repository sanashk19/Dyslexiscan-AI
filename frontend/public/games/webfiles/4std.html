Teacher Guide

Level 5 (Time Master): Ask the child to match the target time first, then choose A.M. or P.M.. Use the background change to discuss day vs night.

Level 6 (Pizza Fractions): Connect slices to parts of a whole. Explain that 2/4 and 1/2 are the same idea (half).

Alice's Adventures: Encourage the child to press the yellow voice buttons. After each answer, ask them to explain “why”.

Tip: Keep sessions short (5–10 minutes) and repeat the same level on different days for confidence.
